Embarking on the Game Development Journey!

By A Secret Dino July 30, 2025

Hello everyone and welcome to the very first post on the SecretDino blog! Today marks an exciting new chapter for me: officially diving headfirst into the world of game development. For a long time, I've been fascinated by the intricate mechanics, captivating stories, and immersive experiences that video games offer. Now, it's time to try my hand at creating them.

My journey will start with learning the fundamentals. I plan to explore various game engines, starting with beginner-friendly Godot and then diving straight into Unreal Engine . The initial focus will be on understanding core concepts such as game loops, physics, rendering, and input handling.

I'll be documenting my progress, challenges, and successes right here. Expect posts about my learning resources, small prototypes, design ideas, and maybe even some early artwork. It's going to be a steep learning curve, but I'm incredibly excited about the creative possibilities. Wish me luck, and stay tuned for more updates on this thrilling adventure!

What kind of games do you enjoy playing? Let me know in the comments (if I ever add them!).
